Crispy Baked Chicken Wings

Description

These crispy baked chicken wings are the perfect appetizer or snack for any occasion. They’re easy to make, packed with flavor, and much healthier than deep-fried wings. Whether you prefer them plain, spicy, or tossed in your favorite sauce, these wings will be a crowd-pleaser.

Pair them with your favorite dipping sauces like ranch, blue cheese, or honey mustard for an irresistible treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds (900g) chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Your favorite wing sauce (e.g., buffalo, BBQ, or teriyaki)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top.
  2. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels. This helps them get extra crispy.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the baking powder, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Toss the chicken wings in the mixture until they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the wings on the wire rack in a single layer. Make sure they are not touching each other to allow for even cooking.
  5. Bake the wings for 40-45 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the wings from the oven and toss them in your favorite wing sauce or serve the sauce on the side for dipping.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
