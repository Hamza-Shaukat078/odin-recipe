Classic Italian Pasta

Description

This classic Italian pasta recipe is simple yet bursting with flavor. Featuring al dente pasta tossed in a rich tomato sauce, this dish is perfect for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy dinner. The fresh garlic, olive oil, and basil give it an authentic taste that transports you straight to Italy.

Whether you serve it with a crisp side salad or some garlic bread, this pasta recipe will quickly become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and then drain the pasta.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, chili flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as needed. Add the chopped basil and stir to combine.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and a few extra basil leaves for garnish.
